Online Test for the Scene-Based Audio (Ambisonics) Mastering Study

Welcome to the participants home page for the study: links, details & instructions for the online test can be found here.

Thank you for agreeing to participate in this study. This page is your home page, should you end up closing a window you can find support documents and links to the various interfaces & questionnaires here.

Your welcome email will contain your unique participant ID. Keep a note of this as you will be required to input this ID on the interfaces and questionnaires.

When you are ready to start, press the start link. This should take you on to the pre-screening questionnaire. From here you will automatically be guided through the testing, at the end of each page/section the next page/section should automatically appear. If this does not happen, refer back to this home page and find the section you have just completed in the testing process. Then find the next section you were due to complete and follow the relevant link. An introductory video will be presented to you during the pre-screening, all details will be discussed in this video.

The testing process will be in the following order:

    Pre-Screening (presented as a questionnaire)
    Part One - Perception
    Short Break (max 5 mins)
    Part Two - Questionnaire Preference
    Post-Screening (included at the end of the Part Two Questionnaire)
